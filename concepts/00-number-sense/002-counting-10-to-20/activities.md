# Activities: Counting 10 to 20

Practical activities to lock in numbers 10 to 20.

---

## Activity 1: The Bundling Game (Off-screen)
* **Goal**: Bundle ten items and add extras.
* **How to play**: Get a packet of rubber bands and some toothpicks/twigs/crayons. Count out 10 items and wrap a rubber band around them. Explain: "This bundle is 10." Now place some individual crayons next to it (e.g. 4 crayons). Ask: "10 and 4 more make what?" Count them together starting from 10: "10... 11, 12, 13, 14!"

---

## Activity 2: Teen Walk (Physical)
* **Goal**: Verbal sequence reinforcement.
* **How to play**: Take a walk outside. Choose a starting point and walk 20 steps, calling out each step number loudly. Try to walk slowly on the teen steps, emphasizing: "Thirteen! Fourteen! Fifteen!"
