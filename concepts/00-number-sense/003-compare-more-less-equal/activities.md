# Activities: Compare More, Less, Equal

Offline visual matching games.

---

## Activity 1: Share the Treats (Off-screen)
* **Goal**: Balance quantities.
* **How to play**: Give your child 6 crackers and yourself 4. Ask: "Is this fair? Who has more?" Let them adjust the crackers until both piles are equal: "Now they are the same (equal)!"

---

## Activity 2: Tower Battle (Visual)
* **Goal**: Measure height vs quantity.
* **How to play**: Build two Lego towers. One tower has 7 wide blocks, the other has 5 thin blocks. Even if the heights look similar, count the blocks to see which has more blocks.
