# Activities: Match Number to Quantity

Active offline numeral connection games.

---

## Activity 1: The Parking Lot (Off-screen)
* **Goal**: Match cars to numbered parking spaces.
* **How to play**: Draw a "parking lot" on a piece of paper with spaces numbered 1 to 6. Have your child park the correct number of toy cars in each numbered space (e.g. park 3 cars in space #3).

---

## Activity 2: Plate Match (Sorting)
* **Goal**: Match physical counts to labels.
* **How to play**: Place 5 paper plates on the floor. Write digits 1 to 5 on cards. Hand the cards to your child, and have them place the matching card next to the plate that has the correct amount of crackers or grapes.
