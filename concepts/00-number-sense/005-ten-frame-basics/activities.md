# Activities: Ten Frame Basics

Practical ten-frame grid games.

---

## Activity 1: The Egg Carton Game (Off-screen)
* **Goal**: Build physical ten-frame structures.
* **How to play**: Cut a standard egg carton to a 2x5 grid of 10 holes. Give your child 10 marbles or beans. Roll a die or call out a number, and have them place that many marbles in the carton. Remind them to start from the top-left and fill the top row first.

---

## Activity 2: Speed Flash (Subitising)
* **Goal**: Instant number recognition.
* **How to play**: Fill a ten-frame grid with a few counters (e.g. 4 counters). Show it to your child for 2 seconds, then cover it up. Ask: "How many dots did you see?" Practice so they can identify 1–5 dots instantly without counting.
