# Activities: Number Line 0 to 10

Hands-on number line games.

---

## Activity 1: The Chalk Hop (Physical)
* **Goal**: Jump along a physical number line.
* **How to play**: Draw a line on the sidewalk with chalk, marking lines from 0 to 10. Write the numbers. Tell your child to stand at 0. Say: "Hop 3 times!" They hop: "1, 2, 3!" Ask: "Which number did you land on?" Now say: "Hop 2 more steps!" Let them count as they hop to 5.

---

## Activity 2: The Missing Link (Visual Card Sort)
* **Goal**: Understand ordinal relationships (before/after/between).
* **How to play**: Write numbers 0 to 10 on index cards. Place them in order, but remove three numbers (e.g. 2, 5, 8). Place them randomly nearby. Ask your child to look at the gaps and slide the correct cards back into place.
