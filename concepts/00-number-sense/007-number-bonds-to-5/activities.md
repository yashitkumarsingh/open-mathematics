# Activities: Number Bonds to 5

Offline part-whole partitioning activities.

---

## Activity 1: The Fist Game (Off-screen)
* **Goal**: Solve for hidden parts.
* **How to play**: Take 5 small items (like beans or beads). Hide them all in your two closed fists. Open one fist to show some of the beans (e.g. 2 beans). Ask your child: "How many are hidden in my other hand to make 5?" Open your hand to verify. Take turns letting your child hide the items!

---

## Activity 2: Five-Frame Two-Color (Visual)
* **Goal**: Visualize splits.
* **How to play**: Draw a grid of 5 squares on a sheet of paper. Use two different colored pencils or blocks. Let your child place counters of different configurations on the grid (e.g. 3 red blocks and 2 yellow blocks) and write down the numbers: "3 and 2 make 5." Repeat with other split arrangements (4 and 1, 5 and 0).
