# Activities: Number Bonds to 10

Physical games to reinforce bonds of 10.

---

## Activity 1: Coat Hanger Pegs (Off-screen)
* **Goal**: Partition 10 items.
* **How to play**: Take a standard clothes hanger and clip exactly 10 wooden clothes pegs onto the bottom rail. Slide them into two separate groups. Have your child count: "6 on this side, 4 on that side. 6 and 4 make 10!" Slide them in different ways (8 and 2, 5 and 5, 9 and 1) to explore all combinations.

---

## Activity 2: The Ten-Finger Flash (Quick Fire)
* **Goal**: Verbal and muscle memory bonds.
* **How to play**: Hold up all 10 fingers. Say: "I'm bending down 4 fingers!" Bend down 4 fingers. Ask: "How many are still standing?" (6). "So 4 and 6 make 10!" Let your child flash fingers at you and ask the questions.
