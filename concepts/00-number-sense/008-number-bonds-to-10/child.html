Number Bonds to 10

Combinaciones Numéricas hasta 10

10 तक संख्या बंधन

🏠 Parent Dashboard

💡 What this concept means

Number bonds to 10 are pairs of numbers that combine to make exactly 10 (e.g. 8+2, 7+3). Spotting these combinations is a critical skill for mental math, enabling strategies like "make-a-ten" when adding larger numbers later (e.g., adding 8+6 is seen as 8+2+4).

❓ Common Misconceptions to watch for

  • Missing Addend Confusion: Confusing the target sum (10) with the added part. They hear "make 10" and represent 10 instead of the missing portion.
  • Bridging Five: Finding it harder to calculate pairs when both numbers are not visualised together. A ten-frame makes the missing part visible immediately as empty squares.

🏡 Off-screen Hands-on Activities

  • Clothes Hanger Pegs: Clip 10 pegs on a coat hanger. Slide them into two groups (e.g., 6 and 4) to visually prove they make 10.
  • Ten Fingers: Hold up 10 fingers. Bend down 3 fingers. Ask: "How many are standing? (7) How many are bent? (3) So 7 and 3 make 10!"

💡 Lo que significa este concepto

Las combinaciones numéricas hasta 10 son parejas de números que se combinan para hacer exactamente 10. Identificar estas combinaciones es clave para el cálculo mental posterior.

❓ Errores comunes a tener en cuenta

  • Falta de soporte visual: Encontrar difícil calcular la diferencia. Un marco de diez ayuda al mostrar las celdas vacías como la parte faltante.

🏡 Actividades prácticas fuera de pantalla

  • Pinzas en percha: Coloca 10 pinzas en una percha. Deslízalas en dos grupos (p. ej., 6 y 4) para comprobar que suman 10.

💡 इस विषय का अर्थ क्या है

10 तक संख्या बंधन संख्याओं के जोड़े हैं जो मिलकर ठीक 10 बनाते हैं (जैसे 8+2, 7+3)। इन संयोजनों को पहचानना मानसिक गणित के लिए एक महत्वपूर्ण कौशल है।
