# Activities: Zero as a Number

Offline games explaining the concept of zero.

---

## Activity 1: The Empty Plate (Off-screen)
* **Goal**: Match number labels to counts, including zero.
* **How to play**: Get three bowls. Put 3 strawberries in the first bowl, 1 strawberry in the second, and leave the third bowl empty. Write digits 0, 1, 3 on sticky notes. Let your child match the stickers to the bowls, explaining: "The empty bowl gets the number 0 because there are zero strawberries!"

---

## Activity 2: The Disappearing Game (Action)
* **Goal**: Understand zero through subtraction.
* **How to play**: Build a tower of 4 Lego blocks. Tell your child: "Take away 1 block. How many are left?" (3). "Take away another. How many?" (2). Repeat until the tower is completely gone. Ask: "How many blocks are left now?" Guide them to say "Zero blocks!"
