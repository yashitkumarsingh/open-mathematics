# Activities: Mixed Mastery Test

Offline synthesized challenge games.

---

## Activity 1: The Kitchen Decathlon (Off-screen)
* **Goal**: Rotate through three distinct tasks in sequence.
* **How to play**: Set up three stations at the table:
  1. **Station 1**: A bowl of spoons (Ask: "Put 4 spoons on the plate.")
  2. **Station 2**: A drawing of a T-chart (Ask: "Split these 5 beans into two groups.")
  3. **Station 3**: Two plates of crackers (Ask: "Which plate has fewer crackers?")
  Let the child go through the circuit step-by-step.

---

## Activity 2: The Action Mix (Physical)
* **Goal**: Switch physical counts.
* **How to play**: Say: "Hop 3 times! Now clap 5 times! Now show me zero fingers!" Check their response speed and accuracy as they shift between the numbers.
