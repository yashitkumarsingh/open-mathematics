Mixed Mastery Test

Prueba de Maestría Mixta

मिश्रित महारत परीक्षण

🏠 Parent Dashboard

💡 What this concept means

This is a cumulative review that mixes various Stage 0 concepts (counting, ten-frames, number lines, comparison, number bonds). Solving mixed problems (interleaved practice) helps lock skills into long-term memory rather than just short-term recitation.

❓ Common Misconceptions to watch for

  • Context Switch Friction: Young children might struggle initially when switching from counting apples to clicking numbers on a line. Give them time to read each prompt.
  • Fatigue: A 10-question test is longer than average. Support your child, encourage them, and take breaks if they get tired. There are no timers.

🏡 Off-screen Hands-on Activities

  • The Kitchen Decathlon: Set up 3 stations at a table. Station 1 asks them to count spoons, Station 2 asks them to split 5 beans into two parts, and Station 3 asks which bowl has more berries. Let them complete the circuit.
  • Flash Cards Mix: Shuffle cards of various Stage 0 off-screen activities and play them in a random order.

💡 Lo que significa este concepto

Esta es una revisión acumulativa que mezcla varios conceptos de la Etapa 0. Resolver problemas combinados ayuda a consolidar las habilidades en la memoria a largo plazo.

❓ Errores comunes a tener en cuenta

  • Fatiga: Una prueba de 10 preguntas es más larga de lo habitual. Anime a su hijo y tome descansos. No hay límites de tiempo.

🏡 Actividades prácticas fuera de pantalla

  • El decatlón de cocina: Configura 3 estaciones. Estación 1: contar cucharas. Estación 2: dividir 5 frijoles en dos partes. Estación 3: comparar cuál plato tiene más frutas.
