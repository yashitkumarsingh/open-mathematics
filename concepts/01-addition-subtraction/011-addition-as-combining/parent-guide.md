# Parent Guide: Addition as Combining / Guía para Padres: Sumar al Combinar

This guide helps you teach your child how to join two sets of items to understand basic addition.

---

## 🇺🇸 English Guide

### ⏰ The 2-Minute Concept Explanation
Addition is often introduced in two ways: **combining** (putting two groups together) and **counting on** (starting at a number and counting forward). This lesson focuses on *combining*. 
When children combine, they look at two separate parts (e.g. 3 apples and 2 pears) and count them to find the whole (5 fruit). The ten-frame grid helps them see this clearly using two different colored dots.

### 🎲 The 5-Minute Everyday Activity
**Items needed**: Spoons and forks, or two different colored toys.
1. Place 3 spoons on the left side of the table and 2 forks on the right.
2. Ask: *"How many spoons? (3) How many forks? (2)"*
3. Push them together in the middle and ask: *"How many utensils do we have in total if we combine them?"*
4. Have them count the combined set: *"1, 2, 3, 4, 5 utensils."*

### 🚀 The 10-Minute Extension Challenge
1. Place 4 green blocks and 3 red blocks on the table.
2. Ask the child to write or say the equation: *"4 + 3 = 7."*
3. Swap the groups (place red first, then green) and ask: *"Does 3 + 4 still make 7?"* Explain that order doesn't change the combined total.

## 🇪🇸 Guía en Español

### ⏰ Explicación Conceptual de 2 Minutos
La suma se introduce de dos maneras: **combinar** (unir dos grupos) y **contar hacia adelante** (comenzar en un número y avanzar). Esta lección se centra en *combinar*. 
Cuando los niños combinan, ven dos partes separadas (p. ej., 3 manzanas y 2 peras) y las cuentan para encontrar el todo (5 frutas).

### 🎲 Actividad Práctica de 5 Minutos
1. Coloca 3 cucharas a la izquierda de la mesa y 2 tenedores a la derecha.
2. Pregunta: *"¿Cuántas cucharas? (3) ¿Cuántos tenedores? (2)"*
3. Únelos en el centro y pregunta: *"¿Cuántos cubiertos tenemos en total si los combinamos?"*
4. Pídeles que cuenten el conjunto combinado: *"1, 2, 3, 4, 5 cubiertos."*

## 🇮🇳 हिन्दी गाइड

### ⏰ 2-मिनट की अवधारणा व्याख्या
जोड़ को अक्सर दो तरीकों से पेश किया जाता है: **संयोजन या मिलाना** (दो समूहों को एक साथ रखना) और **आगे गिनना** (एक संख्या से शुरू करना और आगे बढ़ना)। यह पाठ *मिलाने* पर केंद्रित है।
जब बच्चे मिलाते हैं, तो वे दो अलग-अलग हिस्सों (उदा. 3 चम्मच और 2 कांटे) को देखते हैं और पूरे कुल योग (5) को खोजने के लिए उन्हें गिनते हैं।
