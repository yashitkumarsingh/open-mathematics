# Parent Guide: Counting On / Guía para Padres: Contar Hacia Adelante

This guide helps you teach your child how to add by starting at a number and counting forward.

---

## 🇺🇸 English Guide

### ⏰ The 2-Minute Concept Explanation
When children first learn to add, they usually count everything. If you show them 5 blocks and then add 3 more, they will count *"1, 2, 3, 4, 5... 6, 7, 8."*
**Counting on** is a faster, more mature mental strategy. Instead of starting from 1, the child holds the number 5 in their head, points to the next blocks, and says *"5... 6, 7, 8."* Using a number line helps them see this as taking steps forward.

### 🎲 The 5-Minute Everyday Activity
**Items needed**: A standard ruler or tape measure, and a coin or toy token.
1. Place the token on the number 4 on the ruler.
2. Tell your child: *"We are at 4. Let's add 3. Can you hop the token forward 3 spaces?"*
3. Have them touch each mark as they hop: *"1 hop (lands on 5), 2 hops (lands on 6), 3 hops (lands on 7)!"*
4. Ask: *"What is 4 plus 3? (7)."*

### 🚀 The 10-Minute Extension Challenge
1. Roll a six-sided die. Say that number aloud (e.g. *"6"*).
2. Draw a card numbered 1 to 3 (e.g. *"2"*).
3. Ask your child to start at the die number and count on the card number: *"6... 7, 8!"*
4. Practise starting with the larger number first, showing that $2 + 6$ is easier if you start at 6 and count on 2.

## 🇪🇸 Guía en Español

### ⏰ Explicación Conceptual de 2 Minutos
Contar hacia adelante es una estrategia mental más rápida. En lugar de volver a contar desde 1, el niño mantiene el número inicial en su mente (p. ej., 5) y cuenta hacia adelante: *"5... 6, 7, 8"*. La recta numérica ayuda a visualizar esto como dar pasos hacia adelante.

### 🎲 Actividad Práctica de 5 Minutos
1. Coloca una ficha en el número 4 de una regla.
2. Di: *"Estamos en el 4. Vamos a sumar 3. ¿Puedes hacer que la ficha dé 3 saltos hacia adelante?"*
3. Pídeles que cuenten los saltos: *"4... 5 (primer salto), 6 (segundo salto), 7 (tercer salto)"*.
4. Pregunta: *"¿Cuánto es 4 más 3? (7)"*.

## 🇮🇳 हिन्दी गाइड

### ⏰ 2-मिनट की अवधारणा व्याख्या
आगे गिनना एक तेज़ और परिपक्व मानसिक रणनीति है। 1 से शुरू करने के बजाय, बच्चा अपने दिमाग में पहली संख्या (उदा. 5) रखता है और आगे गिनता है: *"5... 6, 7, 8।"* संख्या रेखा का उपयोग करने से उन्हें यह समझने में मदद मिलती है कि संख्या रेखा पर आगे बढ़ना ही जोड़ है।
