# Australian Curriculum V9 Mapping (Foundation Year)

This document maps the **Open Mathematics Foundation** (Stage 0: Number Sense) to the Australian Curriculum Version 9.0 Mathematics outcomes for the **Foundation** year level.

---

## Foundation Curriculum Standards (Number & Algebra)

### AC9MFN01 (Establish understanding of number names and quantities to 20)
- *Descriptor*: Connect number names, numerals and quantities, including zero, initially up to 10 and then to 20.
- **Mapped Concepts**:
  - `001-counting-0-to-10`
  - `002-counting-10-to-20`
  - `004-match-number-quantity`
  - `009-zero-as-number`

### AC9MFN02 (Subitise small collections)
- *Descriptor*: Subitise small collections of objects up to 5.
- **Mapped Concepts**:
  - `005-ten-frame-basics` (visual patterns of 1–5 dots)

### AC9MFN03 (Compare and order collections)
- *Descriptor*: Compare and order collections, and identify collections with more, fewer (less) or the same number of elements.
- **Mapped Concepts**:
  - `003-compare-more-less-equal`
  - `006-number-line-0-to-10`

### AC9MFN04 (Represent part-whole relationships of numbers up to 10)
- *Descriptor*: Partition and combine collections, and describe part-whole relationships up to 10.
- **Mapped Concepts**:
  - `007-number-bonds-to-5`
  - `008-number-bonds-to-10`
