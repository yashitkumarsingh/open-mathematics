# Common Core State Standards (CCSS) Mapping - Kindergarten

This document maps the **Open Mathematics Foundation** (Stage 0: Number Sense) to the USA Common Core State Standards for Mathematics at the **Kindergarten (K)** level.

---

## Kindergarten Domains & Standards

### 1. Counting & Cardinality (K.CC)

* **CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.K.CC.A.1, K.CC.A.3**: Count to 100 by ones and tens. Write numbers from 0 to 20. Represent a number of objects with a written numeral.
  - **Concepts**: `001-counting-0-to-10`, `002-counting-10-to-20`, `004-match-number-quantity`, `009-zero-as-number`
* **CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.K.CC.B.4**: Understand the relationship between numbers and quantities; connect counting to cardinality.
  - **Concepts**: `001-counting-0-to-10` (one-to-one correspondence)
* **CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.K.CC.B.5**: Count to answer "how many?" questions about as many as 20 things.
  - **Concepts**: `001-counting-0-to-10`, `002-counting-10-to-20`
* **CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.K.CC.C.6**: Identify whether the number of objects in one group is greater than, less than, or equal to the number of objects in another group.
  - **Concepts**: `003-compare-more-less-equal`
* **CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.K.CC.C.7**: Compare two numbers between 1 and 10 presented as written numerals.
  - **Concepts**: `003-compare-more-less-equal`, `006-number-line-0-to-10`

### 2. Operations & Algebraic Thinking (K.OA)

* **CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.K.OA.A.1**: Represent addition and subtraction with objects, fingers, mental images, drawings, sounds, acting out situations, verbal explanations, expressions, or equations.
  - **Concepts**: `007-number-bonds-to-5`, `008-number-bonds-to-10`
* **CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.K.OA.A.3**: Decompose numbers less than or equal to 10 into pairs in more than one way (e.g. 5 = 2 + 3 and 5 = 4 + 1).
  - **Concepts**: `007-number-bonds-to-5`, `008-number-bonds-to-10`
* **CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.K.OA.A.4**: For any number from 1 to 9, find the number that makes 10 when added to the given number.
  - **Concepts**: `008-number-bonds-to-10`
